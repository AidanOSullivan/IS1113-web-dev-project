About Mist

About Mist Mist is a cloud services vendor company set up in 2018 by, then Cork student, now CEO Aidan O’Sullivan.
Although Mist does not have any formal headquarters (due to its business of cloud computing), it’s official
location of operations is considered to be the place it was founded – Cork, Ireland.




The Goal

The vision was clear from the beginning: to provide cloud services with a click of a button to those who need it. O’Sullivan has, from the start,
focused heavily on a modern, sleek and convenient design for his website, letting as many people as possible access the ‘world of the cloud’.
His long-term hope is to make cloud services more ingrained in our every day lives, so much so that we don’t even know we’re using them.




Our CEO

Known to his friends as a light-hearted and likeable person, Aidan O’Sullivan is highly regarded in the business world as a well-rounded,
entrepreneurial-minded and driven individual. Once he finsishes his current college degree, BIS in University College Cork, Aidan hopes to
spearhead the cloud services industry with Mist.
